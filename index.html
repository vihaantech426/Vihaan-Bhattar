The Developer behind the ideas

Hi, I'm Vihaan Bhattar — an independent Android developer passionate about turning ideas into polished, useful mobile applications that people genuinely enjoy using.

I design, develop, and deploy Android applications end-to-end. From initial concept through Play Store publication, I handle every part of the process myself — UI design, backend logic, data handling, and compliance.

My focus is on clean architecture, smooth UX, and meaningful features. Every app I build starts with a real problem I want to solve, and I ship only when I'm proud of the result.

When I'm not coding, I'm exploring the latest Android APIs, experimenting with Kotlin, and writing about my development journey on this blog.

By using any of my Apps, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information I Collect

The specific data collected varies by App. Across my published Apps, I may collect:

  • Information you provide directly: Content you create within the App (e.g., notes, tasks, preferences), account details if registration is offered, and support requests or feedback you send me.
  • Automatically collected information: Device identifiers, operating system version, app version, crash reports and diagnostics, and general usage statistics (e.g., features used, session length).
  • Permissions-based data: Only data corresponding to permissions you explicitly grant (e.g., location for weather features, biometric data processed locally for authentication). Biometric data is never transmitted off your device.

4. Data Retention

I retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. Data stored locally on your device (e.g., notes, tasks) remains under your control and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App or using in-App deletion features.

I implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS), local encryption for sensitive data (AES-256 where applicable), and restricted access controls. Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data; object to or restrict processing; and data portability. To exercise these rights, contact me at vihaanbhattar@gmail.com. I will respond within 30 days.
